Master

Master is a Tamil action-thriller film that revolves around the intense clash between a college professor and a rowdy gangster.

Director Lokesh Kanagaraj
Writers Lokesh Kanagaraj, Rathna Kumar, Pon Parthiban
Stars Vijay, Vijay Sethupathi, Malavika Mohanan




Storyline

Master portrays the power struggle and the battle of ideologies between a college professor, who believes in reforming students, and a gangster, who thrives on chaos and violence.

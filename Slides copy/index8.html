Chennai Express

Chennai Express is a Bollywood action-comedy film that follows the journey of a man who travels from Mumbai to Rameswaram and falls in love with the daughter of a local don.

Director Rohit Shetty
Writers K. Subhash (story), Yunus Sajawal (screenplay)
Stars Shah Rukh Khan, Deepika Padukone, Sathyaraj




Storyline

Chennai Express is a romantic action-comedy film that tells the story of a man's journey filled with humorous and adventurous encounters as he travels to fulfill a promise made to his grandmother and ends up falling in love.

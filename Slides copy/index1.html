After the events of Avengers: Endgame, the universe is once again in danger. The Avengers must assemble once more to face their greatest threat yet in the Secret Wars.

Avengers Secret Wars follows the story of the Avengers as they face off against a new threat to the universe. With their powers and abilities, they must once again save the world from destruction.

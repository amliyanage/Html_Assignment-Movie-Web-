K.G.F: Chapter 1

K.G.F: Chapter 1 follows the story of a young man named Rocky who rises from the streets of Mumbai to become the powerful ruler of a gold mine in the Kolar fields of Karnataka.

Director Prashanth Neel
Writers Prashanth Neel
Stars Yash, Srinidhi Shetty, Ramachandra Raju




Storyline

K.G.F: Chapter 1 tells the tale of ambition, power, and greed set in the backdrop of the gold mines in Karnataka. It follows the journey of Rocky from rags to riches, as he fights against all odds to establish his dominance in the ruthless world of gold mining.
